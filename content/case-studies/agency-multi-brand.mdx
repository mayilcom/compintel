## Background

A performance marketing agency managing competitive intelligence for 8 CPG clients across FMCG, personal care, and food & beverage categories.

Their process before Mayil: a junior analyst spent 4–5 hours every Friday manually checking Instagram, Meta Ads Library, and Amazon listings for each client's competitors. Results were compiled into a Google Doc and shared in Monday review calls.

Problems with this process: inconsistent coverage, analyst turnover risk, no historical data for trend analysis, and a format that didn't translate well into actionable recommendations.

## What changed

After implementing Mayil across all 8 client accounts, the agency's Friday prep process changed. Instead of manual collection, the team reviewed Sunday briefs for each client account and spent their time on interpretation and strategy — not data gathering.

## The outcome

Client retention improved. Competitive intelligence became a differentiated offering — clients could see their competitors' activity in a structured format that the agency presented in monthly strategy reviews.
